    Project

    This project will aim to investigate whether the United States has become more financially and socially equal in recent years. The 4 chart below look at the development of income equality based on Sex, Race, Education, and Location

    The following charts all use data from FRED (Federal Reserve data base). From these charts, we can draw a conclusion that the US is becoming more financially equal.
